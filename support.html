Q: How do I start the game?
A: After opening the game, tap the "Draw Balls" button to change the balls on the track. Click on your preferred ball and skill, then tap "Race Start" to begin the game.
Q: How do I purchase in-game items?
A: Tap the "Store" icon in the main menu, select the item you wish to purchase, and complete the payment through the App Store. All purchases are processed through Apple's official payment system for security.
Q: Does the game require an internet connection?
A: This is an offline single-player game and does not require an internet connection to play. However, an internet connection is needed when making in-app purchases.
Q: How do I restore purchases?
A: If you've changed devices or reinstalled the game, please tap the "Restore Purchases" button on the store page and sign in with the same Apple ID to restore your previous purchases.
Q: What should I do if the game crashes or has issues?
A: Please try the following steps:
  • Restart the game
  • Ensure your iOS system is up to date
  • Check for game updates
  • If the problem persists, please contact us via email with your device model and iOS version
Q: How do I request a refund?
A: All purchases are processed through the App Store. For refunds, please visit Apple's refund page or request a refund through your "Purchase History" in the App Store app.
